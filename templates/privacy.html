Privacy Policy

What Information Is Collected?

  • Outreach does not store any personal information that you may provide.
  • Any data accessed is used only to generate your playlists.

How Is Your Data Used?

  • Your data is processed in real time to create your playlist.
  • Outreach does not save, share, or sell any of your information.
  • Once your playlist is generated, your data is not retained.

Third-Party Services

  • Outreach uses the Spotify API to access your music data, with your explicit permission.
  • All permissions requested are solely for the purpose of generating your playlist.

Your Control

  • You can revoke the access of this webapp to your Spotify account at any time through your Spotify account settings.
  • Here is a more detailed guide on doing so.

If you have any concerns regarding the privacy control visit the about section and feel free to contact me.
